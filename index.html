Understanding PayPal Goods and Services Fee Calculator

The PayPal goods and services fee calculator is specifically designed for commercial transactions. When you receive payments for goods or services, PayPal typically charges:

  • Domestic transactions: 2.99% + $0.49 fixed fee
  • International transactions: 4.49% + $0.49 fixed fee
  • Micropayments: 6.5% + $0.05 fixed fee (for transactions under $10)

Using our calculator, you can instantly see how much of your payment goes to PayPal fees and how much you actually receive.

Frequently Asked Questions - PayPal Calculator Fee

How do I calculate PayPal fees for goods and services?

To calculate PayPal goods and services fees, multiply your transaction amount by the percentage rate (2.99% for domestic or 4.49% for international), then add the fixed fee ($0.49). For example, on a $100 domestic transaction: ($100 √ó 0.0299) + $0.49 = $3.48 in fees.

What is the PayPal international fee calculator used for?

The PayPal international fee calculator is used to determine fees for cross-border transactions. International transactions have higher fees (4.49% + $0.49) compared to domestic ones, plus potential currency conversion fees.

Can I avoid PayPal fees?

PayPal fees for goods and services transactions cannot be completely avoided as they're part of PayPal's business model. However, you can minimize fees by using micropayment pricing for small transactions or negotiating who pays the fees.

How accurate is this PayPal fee calculator 2026?

Our PayPal fee calculator 2026 is updated with the latest official PayPal fee structure. It provides accurate calculations for standard transactions. However, some specialized account types or promotional rates may differ slightly.

Should I charge customers the PayPal fee?

Whether to charge customers PayPal fees depends on your business model and competition. Some businesses absorb the fees (building them into product prices), while others add them as a separate line item. Use our calculator to determine the exact amount if you choose to pass fees to customers.

Are PayPal micropayment fees better for small transactions?

PayPal micropayment fees (6.5% + $0.05) are typically better for transactions under $10. For example, on a $5 transaction, standard fees would be $0.64, while micropayment fees would be $0.38. You need to contact PayPal to enable micropayment pricing on your account.
